AGAINST SEXUAL HARASSMENT

Hathras Gangrape - Year 2020


The incident took place on 14 September 2020, when the victim, a 19-year-old girl went to a farm to collect cattle fodder.

The victim was dragged away by dupatta around her neck injuring her spinal cord in the process. The girl was allegedly gang-raped by four men. She was left paralyzed with a severe spinal cord injury which doctors said could have been permanently damaged.

According to the police, the victim had been strangled with her dupatta.

She died on 29 September 2020.

Kathua Rape Case - Year 2018


A 8 year old girl was abducted, rape, and murdered in Rasana village near Kathua in Jammu and Kashmir, in January 2018.

The examination by the doctors found that the girl had been drugged with a sedative before she was raped and murdered.The forensic examination stated that girl had been raped multiple times by different men, and that she had been strangled to death, as well as hit in the head with a heavy stone.

Her body was discovered by the villagers a kilometer away from the village, weeks after She disappeared.

Nirbhaya Case - 2012


On 16 December 2012 in Munirka, a neighbourhood in South Delhi. A 23-year-old female physiotherapy intern, was beaten, gang-raped, and tortured in a private bus.

A medical report later said that she suffered serious injuries to her abdomen, intestines and genitals due to the assault, and doctors said that the damage indicated that a blunt object (suspected to be the iron rod) may have been used for penetration.After the beatings and rape ended, the attackers threw both victims from the moving bus.

She died on 29 December,2012 in the hospital.
